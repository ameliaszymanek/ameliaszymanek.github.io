Hi! My name is Amelia. I was born and raised in Milwaukee, WI. I graduated from Loras College in Dubuque, IA with a degree in music education.
While student teaching I auditioned to be a vocalist in the United States Air Force Regional Bands. I won the job, graudated, and headed to Basic Training in San Antonio, TX at Lackland Air Force Base. I ended up staying in Texas and was stationed at the Band of the West.
Singing in the Air Force made for lots of new adventures...including meeting my husband! He was stationed in Illinois at Scott Air Force Base so we were married in the San Antonio Courthouse. We spent the year planning for another wedding celebration with friends and family.
Two graduations, two weddings, countless tours, and one dog later we are studying to be software developers back in the good land, Milwaukee.
